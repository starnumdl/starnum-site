Zavansay

The structured world of Zavansay.

Not a shop. A set of collections, each built around a different kind of moment — designed to be added to, mixed, and grown over time. Six live here today, with more in the works.

Collection

Signature Cards

for the words you mean to keep

Each card is built to hold more than a quick note — heavier stock, a quiet design on the front, and a blank interior that leaves room for what you actually want to say. A gentle prompt system offers a line or two to start from, for the moments when the right words don't come easily.

  • Keepsake card stock
  • Blank interior
  • Gentle prompt cues

Suite Invitations

the suite, not the paperwork

A milestone deserves more than a single sheet. Suite Invitations are built as a set — the main piece, the details your guests need, and the small finishing layers that make an invitation feel considered before it's even opened. Always designed, and referred to, as a suite.

  • Main invitation
  • Detail cards
  • Envelope & liner

Program Fans

held through every word, kept long after

Printed with the order of your ceremony or event, a Program Fan does double duty — useful in the moment, and a small relief from the heat besides. Most are folded away at the end of the night and kept on a shelf for years.

  • Folded fan format
  • Ribbon or cord finish
